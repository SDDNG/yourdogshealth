Us and Our Dogs

We had pets as children and after much pleading from our sons, delaying and procrastination, we got our first dog, Sebastian, when our eldest son was 12 and his younger brother was 9. Sebastian is a Bernese Mountain Dog and now 4 years old, so mature in body if not attitude. Bernese are a giant breed, Sebastian is 50kg approximately, but despite their size, they are famously known for being very soft and gentle. Recently Bernese have gotten a lot of coverage in Ireland (where we live) as the President of Ireland has two. As long as you can manage the giant muddy paws and all-year round shedding, the love you get in return is invaluable. In the last year we added our second dog, Fenrir, a Broholmer (or Danish Mastiff), Fenrir is now 9 months old and weighs around 60kg with about another 10kg to go. Just as with the Bernese, Broholmers are extremely gentle breeds.
