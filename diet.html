They are What You Feed Them (and How Much)!

Just as human nutrition is divided into passionate camps, canine nutrition has multiple factions. Most people choose to feed their dogs dry dog biscuits called kibble but there are those that advocate raw feeding(link) and others who cook various dishes for their dogs. We are agnostic in this regard and think there are many roads to Rome, the main thing is to pay close attention to the physical condition of your dog using the body condition score and if you notice that your dog is losing or gaining too much weight to adjust appropriately. For those interested in raw feeding, here is a very good introductory resource with a calculator to help you initially decide how much food your best friend needs. It’s important to remember while exercise is very important, like us, no dog can outrun a bad diet, but it’s totally within our control as to the amount of food your dog has access to. One final tip, watch out for treats and snacks, dogs have actually evolved to pull at our heartstrings quite effectively, the puppy eyes they give you aren’t accidental.
